Help (using the catalog)

Format:

Search for any type of publishing format that includes your search words

Language:

Select the Language you want results to be in (Selecting “French” will cause only french-language works to be displayed).

Location:

Restrict the search based on where the media is housed (What Library a book is at, for example)

Year:

Select a range of publishing dates to restrict search results to (Selecting 2000-2009 will only provide results from that decade, for instance).

Search:

Press this button to perform search in the library database based on the fields specified by the user.

Clear:

Pressing clear will empty all search fields and reset all drop-down menus. An easy way to reset the search!

Combining terms: Boolean operators: and, or, not

Use or to broaden your search. Example: search words in title for "hardware or software" to find all records containing the word "hardware" as well as all the records containing the word "software" in the title

Use and to limit your search. Example: search words in title for "hardware and software" to find all records containing BOTH the word "hardware" and the word "software" in the title.

Use not to narrow your search. Example: search words in title for "hardware not software" to find all records containing the word "hardware" but not if they also include the word "software" in the title. Be careful, you may eliminate records containing the term "hardware" that are of value to your research!
